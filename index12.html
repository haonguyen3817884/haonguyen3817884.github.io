I started developing an interest in IT when I accidentally watched a video on Youtube which show why children should learn about coding. Since that, I began looking for more information about IT like how IT jobs would be like or what inside IT courses.

Why did you choose to come to RMIT?

There are two reasons I chose RMIT to study IT. High quality teacher is the first reason. RMIT is one of the most expensive universities in Vietnam. It means that the school can pay for well-qualified teachers who have experience of teaching and working on IT field. The final reason is that RMIT provides an international environment. Not only Vietnameses, but a lot of students from other countries like Korea or Australia also study in RMIT. Using english as the main language allows all students from different countries to speak and work together.

What do you expect to learn during your studies?

An understanding of computers and programming languages are two important things that I want to learn during this course. Understanding of computers means that I have to know basic knowledge of computers like how they store data and how they process it. Programming languages refers to common languages like java, C++ or Ruby. it means that I want to learn how to master those languages to build programs.
